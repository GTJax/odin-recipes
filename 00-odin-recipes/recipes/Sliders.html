Roast Beef and Cheddar Sliders

Description

Beef and cheddar sliders are a delightful, bite-sized treat perfect for any gathering.

These mini sandwiches feature juicy, seasoned beef patties topped with melted, sharp cheddar cheese, creating a savory and rich flavor combination. Nestled in soft, slightly toasted slider buns, the patties are often accompanied by caramelized onions and a tangy sauce, adding layers of sweetness and zest.

Each bite-sized slider offers a perfect balance of textures and tastes, making them a crowd-pleasing favorite for parties, game days, or casual dinners.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons of butter, melted, divided
  • 1 (12 roll) package Hawaiian rolls
  • 12 ounces deli roast beef
  • 1/2 cup of BBQ Sauce
  • 1 cup of Cheddar Cheese sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of Onion Powder
  • 2 teaspoons of Everything Bagel Seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Brush the botteom and sides of a 9x13-inch baking dish with melted butter until lightly coated.
  2. Place bottom half of rolls in baking dish and top evenly with roast beef slices. Drizzle BBQ sauce evenly over roast beef and dollop cheese sauce evenly over the top. Place top roll halves on top.
  3. Stir together remaining butter, garlic powder, and onion powder and brush evenly over bun tops. Sprinkly with bagel seasoning.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the center is warm and melted, and bread is toasted and golden brown, 12 to 14 minutes.
