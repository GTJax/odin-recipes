Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

Homemade mac and cheese with a bread crumb topping is a comforting, indulgent dish that combines creamy, cheesy pasta with a satisfying crunch.

The tender macaroni is enveloped in a rich, velvety cheese sauce made from sharp cheddar and creamy Gruy√®re, creating a luscious, savory base. A golden-brown layer of buttery bread crumbs, toasted to perfection, crowns the dish, adding a delightful texture contrast.

Baked until bubbly and slightly crisp on the edges, this classic comfort food delivers a warm, hearty experience with every bite.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of salted butter
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups of milk, or more as needed
  • 2 cups of Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/2 cups of Finely Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt and Ground Black Pepper (Optional)

Bread Crumb Topping

  • 2 tablespoons of Salted Butter
  • 1/2 Cup of Bread Crumbs
  • 1 Pinch of Ground Paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat the overn to 350 degress F (175 degrees C). Greease an 8-inch square dish.
  2. Make the macaroni and cheese: Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add macaroni and simmer, stiring occasionally, under tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; it will fiinish cooking in the oven. Drain and transfer to the prepared baking dish.
  3. While the macaroni is cooking, melt 1/4 cup butter in a medium skillet over low heat. Whisk in flout and stir until the mixture becomes paste-like and light golden brown, 3 to 5 mintues.
  4. Gradually whisk 2 1/2 cups of milk ino the flour mixture, and bring to a simmer. Stir in shredded Cheddar and finely grated Parmesan cheeses; season with salt and pepper. Cook and stire over low heat unitl cheese is melted and sauce has thickened, 3 to 5 minutes, adding up to 1/2 cup or more of milk if needed. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni and stir until well combined.
  5. Make the bread crumb topping: Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add bread crumbs; cook and stir unil well coated and browned. Spread bread crumbs over macaroni and cheese, then sprinkle with paprika.
  6. Bake in the preheated over until topping is golden brown and macaroni and cheese is bubbling, about 30 minutes.
